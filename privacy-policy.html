Privacy Policy for PowerCheckAI

Last updated: April 2026

PowerCheckAI is a tool developed by God’s Electric LLC to assist with electrical diagnostics, job documentation, and workflow support. This Privacy Policy explains what information the PowerCheckAI app collects, how it is used, and how users can request deletion of their data.

Information We Collect

PowerCheckAI may collect the following types of information when the user chooses to provide it:

  • Photos uploaded for diagnostic analysis
  • Job notes or descriptions entered by the user
  • Basic account information (such as email) for login and access

PowerCheckAI does not collect location data, contact lists, device identifiers, or any information unrelated to the app’s core functionality.

How We Use Information

Information provided by the user is used only for:

  • Generating diagnostic insights
  • Supporting job documentation
  • Maintaining user access to the app

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Data Storage

User data is stored securely and is only retained for as long as the user maintains an active PowerCheckAI account.

How to Delete Your Data

Users may delete their PowerCheckAI account and all associated data at any time by visiting:

https://godselectric.com/delete-account

Once a deletion request is submitted, all user data is permanently removed.
